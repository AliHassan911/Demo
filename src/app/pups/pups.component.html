Breeding Methodman to Badger was our plan for a long time, But when Methodman died , Tatanka replaced him and our plan changed to breeding Tatanka to Badger.
Tatanka is a very agile and strong son of Methodman, we have our strong belief on him that he is gonna produce same like his sire.
The purpose of doing this great breeding is their bloodline combinations. As we can see Tatanka is Redboy Jocko Jeep Rascal and Badger is Redboy Jocko Bolio Tombstone with a half quarter of Bommerang blood. We already have seen the successfull combination of these two bloodlines. Also, the reason to breed these two dogs is that Badger is mouth is very hard and tatanka lacks it a little bit, so Badger will add alot more mouth to his progeny. Plus, they both are very deep Gamedogs, so the progeny is also gonna be the very best their Game.
This breeding is planned in February, 2023. I hope we will do it successfully and it will produce some real Gamedogs, Which will be alot human firendly but will be too dangerous for DOGS.
